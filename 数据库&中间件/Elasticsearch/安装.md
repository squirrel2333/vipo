# Mac版下载

<https://www.elastic.co/cn/downloads/elasticsearch>

# 启动

* `./bin/elasticsearch`
* 默认开启security，tls, enrollment, 可以在配置里更改`config/elasticsearch.yml`
* 启动后通过浏览器`https://localhost:9200`访问 `user: elastic password: 会在控制台显示`
